About Us

We are Westcoast Group of Companies


Dedicated to advancing Liberia’s telecommunications, energy, and agriculture sectors. With a strong commitment to innovation and sustainability, we provide solutions that empower local communities and drive economic progress growth.



Our Mission

To lead innovation in Liberia's telecoms, energy, and agricultural sectors while prioritizing sustainability.



Our Vision

To be the foremost provider of integrated solutions that empower communities and enhance quality of life.



Company History

2010: Founded to enhance Liberia's telecommunications infrastructure. 2015: Expanded services to renewable energy solutions. 2020: Introduced sustainable agricultural practices.
